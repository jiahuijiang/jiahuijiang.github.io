Last year, I attended <a href="https://www.instagram.com/_imagima/">Chika</a>'s projection mapping class at ITP Camp. Throughout the class, Chika showcased a variety of projection mapping projects. One particular project that I really loved was Motomichi's <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cEJ7g8VVZcI">Tiny People Tribe"</a>. It's incredibly charming and truly interactive with its surrounding space.

Ever since then, I've been wanting to create my own version. I'm so excited that this is finally made it a reality.

The projection layering is relatively straightforward. I used <a href="https://www.resolume.com/">Resolume</a> Arena for it.
This project is also my first time making animation. I used <a href="https://flipaclip.com/">Flipaclip</a> on iPad and drew each frame. It's such an intuitive and flexible software, and I really appreciate the programmers for their work!

I hope you enjoy the tiny cat's adventure!
